Recent Work


Cargill (2018-19) I worked on a robust, complicated stock hedging platform for the largest privately-held company in America.

As a lead UX consultant I worked with a team of engineers and stakeholders to test and deliver an innovative platform supporting highly complicated financial instruments. I conducted multiple user-testing sessions to validate designs and rapidly iterate on solutions via interactive prototypes.

More designs are available upon request.



Prealize (2017-18) A Stanford-backed startup that uses machine learning to predict health care costs

Formerly Cardinal Analytx - I designed a health care application that uses machine-learning to predict medical costs. I worked closely and regularly with health professionals to research their workflows and iterate on a solution that fitted their working model. A demo of the product was showcased at the 2018 Winter Tech conference in Palo Alto, CA.

Google (2015-16) I designed complex interactions for their email marketing engine.

The product supported 80+ Google products at scale in many languages. I created sketches, flows, designs and prototypes to explore, test and validate designs with great success. I iterated on complex workflows that used solid user research and ux best practices: use-case clarification, qual and quant research, design validation, rapid and iterative design processes.
